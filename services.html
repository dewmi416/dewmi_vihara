Architectural Custom Design

In Sri Lanka, an aquarium is a statement of luxury. We work with local architects and interior designers to integrate living art into your space. From high-clarity Starphire glass to bespoke cabinetry that matches your decor, we use 3D rendering to visualize your project before we break ground.

  • High-Clarity Glass & Custom Acrylic
  • Structural Engineering for Residential Tiling
  • Bespoke Cabinetry & In-Wall Integration

02

Exotic Marine Ecosystems

Leveraging Sri Lanka’s unique position in the marine world, we provide ethically sourced species and laboratory-grown corals. Our biologists ensure that every species—from the local Powder Blue Tang to imported exotic SPS corals—is quarantined and acclimated for long-term health in your ecosystem.

  • Ethically Sourced Reef-Safe Fish
  • Hard and Soft Coral Propagation
  • Strict Quarantine & Health Protocols

Professional Maintenance

Sri Lanka's tropical climate requires expert temperature and salinity control. Our white-glove service handles everything: from Chiller system calibration and ICP water testing to automated dosing. We ensure your reef thrives while you simply enjoy the view.

  • Tropical Climate Chiller Maintenance
  • 24/7 Emergency Support in Colombo & Suburbs
  • Automated Nutrient Control & Water Changes
